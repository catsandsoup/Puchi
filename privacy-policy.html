Privacy Policy for Puchi

Last updated: January 2025

Data Collection and Storage

Puchi is designed with privacy in mind. All your personal data stays on your device:

  • Love notes and journal entries are stored locally on your device
  • Photos and videos are stored locally on your device
  • Location data (when enabled) is stored locally on your device
  • Partner name and preferences are stored locally on your device

Data Sharing

We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data with third parties. Your information never leaves your device.

Permissions

Puchi may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: To capture photos for your love notes (optional)
  • Photo Library: To select existing photos for your love notes (optional)
  • Location: To automatically tag your notes with location (optional)

All permissions are optional and can be denied without affecting core functionality.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features including device encryption and access controls.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual notes within the app or delete all data by uninstalling the app from your device.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
